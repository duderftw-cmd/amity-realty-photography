How do I pay for the shoot?

expand_more

Payment for the shoot is due upon delivery of the photos. You will use Spiro to pay for and download the photos, which allows you to preview the branded versions prior to payment. Once the invoice is paid, Spiro will provide the unbranded, downloadable versions of the photos.

How long will the shoot take?

expand_more

Photos of a 2500 SQFT home take about an hour. If you are having a 3D Virtual Tour taken as well you can expect the shoot to take an additional hour.

What if the weather is bad during the shoot time?

expand_more

If it's looking like it’s going to be a rainy day we may need to reschedule the shoot for another time, however even if there’s gray skies in your photos, our editing team typically adds bright blue skies in post production so the weather does not need to be perfect for your photos to turn out beautifully!
